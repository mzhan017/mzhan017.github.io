# [AI] Security

[The Power of AI: Can We Only Let AI Thrive Even More? ](2026-3-14.md) <br>
This article states that in the course of AI's prosperity, we need to further boost AI's development to mitigate the various risks it brings. Among all these risks, security is the foremost one. While learning about security issues is no easy feat, this is definitely a promising direction for the continued prosperity of the industry. It may even be a career shift option for those who face unemployment in the future.

With technological innovation, security issues are evolving in tandem. The advent of the network gave rise to network security problems; the boom of the Internet brought about various injection attacks and Trojan horse attacks; the hardware upgrades and performance optimization through hardware programming of CPUs led to the analysis of various CPU security vulnerabilities; the emergence of cloud services gave birth to cloud service security issues; and the development of cloud-native technology has resulted in cloud-native-related security challenges. Unsurprisingly, with the rise and prosperity of AI, AI security has become a major research focus. Recently, I learned that Google has acquired an Israel-based security company to place a strategic bet on this field (https://www.googlecloudpresscorner.com/2026-03-11-Google-Completes-Acquisition-of-Wiz).
